Our Programs

What We Teach!

Toddler&Me

Turn on that music and see what your toddler's got! Parents, dance with your child too!!

Preschool

Students dance at their own pace and learn the fundamentals of being an engaging dancer.

Beginner 1

Students are taught constistency, balance, coordination, and muscle memory for dancing.

Beginner 2

Students in this session will focus on competitive dancing in a team environment.

Intermediate

Dancers will focus on performing in sync, and learning how to get a crowd on their feet!

Advanced

Advanced dancers will compete at the highest level, and can also help us choreograph signature moves!
