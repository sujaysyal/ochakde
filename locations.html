Our Story


Ochakde was founded by Sumeet Syal in March of 2020. His mission is build a community for those who are passionate about the variety of types of Indian Dance. Sumeet has competed in collegiate Bhangra, and ever since dancing has become an integral part of his life journey. He feels that more than a hobby, dancing is something that helps him unwind from his high-stess corporate job. He wants to the true value of dancing with students at Ochakde by turning them into stellar performers. Sujay has two boys, Sujay and Sachin, who are both significantly contributing to the organization. Sujay is a student at Irvington High School and has participated in Bhangra Empire programs and was a two time class MVP. He also developed this website from stratch using programming. Sachin is an advanced level dancer in Bhangra Empire and is leading the Ochakde's marketing and public relations. After the Syal's looked at the dance opportunites offered in their community, they noticed that majority of programs were not accommodating the wide variety of skills levels from their dancers. As a result, more advanced dancers would pick up moves quickly and perform well, whereas those who needed more support were not inspired by their experiences. Sumeet decided this is an opportunity opportunity to create a program where students feel comfortable and passionate.
