Who We Are?


O'Chakde is an organization to empower students from a variety of communites through dance. More than teaching classes and allowing our students to perform at dance offs, we are building a strong Indian community and will unite people from many different areas in this extremely diverse country. We specialize in Bollywood Dance along with classical dances like Bhangra. Not only do we offer 3 free classes so you can truly understand what we are all about, we also have classes for adults so don't be shy to express your inner dancer and register now!!!

Our Services



Traditional

Students can choose a variety of traditional dances they want to learn from all around India. O'Chakde is dedicated to including people from a variety of cultural backgrounds, some of our traditional dances are Bhangra and Dandiya.

Bollywood

Bollywood is a rapidly changing industry that is combining traditional Indian and hip hop music. We are so excited to offer Bollywood programs because it will encourage us to continuously come up with fresh dance moves. Learn more on the Missions tab.

Performance

The most exciting part of any dance program is the dance-off itself!! Our ultimate goal is to insipre students through dancing and help their confidence levels skyrocket. Not only will we transform you into a great dance, but also a stellar overall performer!
